Our Roots & History

Established in 1782:
The Mysore Legacy

"For over two centuries, Sultan Market has preserved Mysore's sovereign standard of luxury trade under direct imperial principles."

Sultan Market was founded in 1782, a pivotal era when the Tiger of Mysore, Tipu Sultan, championed innovative regional craft alliances. By giving official sovereign protections to weaver and carver guilds, Mysore's finest exports were guarded from generic, cheap mass-production.

This digital vault brings these centuries of hand-finished authenticity straight to refined houses around the globe.
